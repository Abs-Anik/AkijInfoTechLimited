Specification

Specification Details
Brand Hewlett-Packard Enterprise (HPE)
Country of Origin U.S.A
Model Aruba AP-505 Wi-Fi 6 Indoor Access Points
AP Type Indoor
Wi-Fi Radio Specifications
  • Dual radio, 5 GHz and 2.4 GHz 802.11ax 2x2 MIMO
  • Up to 256 associated client devices per radio and 16 BSSIDs per radio
  • 5GHz radio: Up to 1.2 Gbps wireless data rate with 2SS HE80 802.11ax client devices
  • 2.4GHz radio: Up to 574 Mbps (287 Mbps) wireless data rate with 2SS HE40 (HE20) 802.11ax client devices
Supported Radio Technologies
  • 802.11b: DSSS
  • 802.11a/g/n/ac: OFDM
  • 802.11ax: OFDMA with up to 8 resource units
Wi-Fi Antennas
  • Two integrated dual-band downtilt omni-directional antennas for 2x2 MIMO
  • Peak antenna gain of 4.9 dBi in 2.4 GHz and 5.7 dBi in 5 GHz
  • Optimized for horizontal ceiling mounted orientation
Certifications
  • Wi-Fi CERTIFIED a, b, g, n, ac
  • Wi-Fi CERTIFIED 6 (ax)
  • WPA, WPA2 and WPA3
  • Bluetooth SIG and Wi-Fi CERTIFIED Location™
Other Interfaces and Features
  • Auto-sensing link speed (10/100/1000BASE-T) and MDI/MDX
  • 802.3az Energy Efficient Ethernet (EEE)
  • Factory reset, LED mode control (normal/off)
  • Visual indicators for system and radio status
Power Sources
  • Supports Power over Ethernet (PoE)
  • Maximum power consumption in idle mode: 4.3W (DC) or 6.2W (PoE)
  • Maximum power consumption in deep-sleep mode: 1.7W (DC) or 3.7W (PoE)
Wi-Fi Features
  • Transmit Beamforming
  • Passpoint Wi-Fi (Release 2) (Hotspot 2.0)
  • Dynamic Frequency Selection (DFS)
  • Maximum Ratio Combining (MRC)
  • Cyclic Delay/Shift Diversity (CDD/CSD)
  • Space-Time Block Coding
  • Low-Density Parity Check (LDPC)
